Who are we?

We are a small but very effective team of highly educated people coming from different professions and with a great knowledge of the tourism sector. We have the right mentality and all the skills necessary to tailor the best tours in Rome ever. So far, based on years of experience and our own travels around the world we built, ran and maintained a five star award winning agency. Our philosophy and eye for details have proven to be a base around which we were able to tailor the best impression of Rome.

Who we are not?

We are definitely not the part of the same old Roman tourism offer that wants to charge you a lot of money for taking you to places where everybody goes and which you can find on your own anyway. We are not organising tours in Rome where you can hear cheezy stories about gladiators and glory of Roman empire over and over. We are not going to put you in a group of fifty (or more!) people with headphones that follow a tour guide carrying a banner. We are not trying to sell you an illusion of greatness that has never been for sale in the first place.

We are not goint to waste yours or our time.

Our History

While pursuing her PhD in Art History in Italy, our founder Mrs. Nina Marok realised that her true passion lies far away from academy career. She actually found out that organising events that make people happy is her true fulfillment. So she entered the tour business as an Italian tour guide working for major tour operators in Rome and, as a result of her talent and commitment, soon she was able to build a reputation in the sector.

She realized that the processes and approach to clients could to be improved. Not just a little bit, but at the revolutionary level. Many things needed to be changed. From simple organisational tasks to general philosophy, all of it. So she changed it.

Even though being a part of a big company has its benefits, it often means that the services you provide lack a depth of a human touch. So, she decided to start Sublime Tours, a tour operator that will always value the true idea of hospitality before anything else. It started as a one person agency. Nina was guiding tours, creating itineraries, managing relations with providers, creating promotions… Fortunately, it did not take long before she found the rest of us - other guides who share the same values, best local chefs, coolest restaurant owners and even the administrative support.

why would you book a tour in Rome with us?

We are experts of Rome. None of us is here for any other reason than a true and unconditional love for the Eternal city. We know exactly why you came here and what you are looking for. We know it because we have dedicated our lives to that very same thing.

The warm, pleasurable, tasty, almost snobby but ever humble, and above all - deeply romantic feeling of streets of Rome. Places like you see nowhere else, anywhere, anytime. Built by the greatest artists of mankind and preserved through centuries, some through millenniums. Only to show the divine glory of human ability to create infinite beauty.

And the food, oh the FOOD...

The mouth full of delicious food, the perfect harmony of flavours. And that mind blowing sensation of best Italian wines. Sweet delicacies made in heaven, crafted through centuries by immortal Italian masters. You taste it and you ask yourself - is it offered to us so that in this life we too could have a chance to understand the TRUE meaning of ‘La Dolce Vita’?

All those cool and fancy restaurants created in your dreams. Lovable and charming waiters and chefs full of unexplainable character and hospitality. All those beautiful people in the beautiful city.

And the warm breeze from Tyrrhenian sea mixing with the mountain air from the North. It cools off those beautiful hot evenings and takes all the bad things away. The exactly same breeze that rejuvenated and inspired emperors. Did you know that nowadays it can only be felt at certain spots in the city? Do you want to know why?

Rome is a living history carved in stone. The experience like no other. Only fool would not like it.

And, by the way, alongs the way you will have a chance to learn secrets of pasta, pizza, tiramisu, gelato and even the coffee masters. Stuff that you cannot find in online videos. Real secrets.

Yes, we can and we know how to give you just that.

That’s why you should choose us.

Our philosophy

The main point is in the fact that we have decided to see our customers as our guests rather than mere clients. When you are on vacation in a foreign country, you often feel that you are spending a lot of your valuable time trying to find out what the best experience might be, sometimes probably thinking that you could be having much better time if you had family or friends to show you around. What if that is not impossible? Well, this is where our approach excels.

We believe that your best experience in Italy starts from the moment you land on this page. We can make it easy for you. You will easily get all the information you need. You can stop worrying about the little details that sometimes present a stone in your shoe. In all our tours and cooking classes in Rome we successfully removed all those unnecessary moments with the goal of providing you with the best experience of Rome and Italian lifestyle ever. Without any further thoughts you can think of us as of your warm welcoming friends.
