Who are we?

We are a small but very effective team of highly educated people coming from different branches and with wide experience in the tourism sector. So far, based on years of experience in organising tours in Rome and our own travels around the world we were able to run and maintain a five star award winning agency. Our philosophy and eye for details have proven to be the base around which we were able to tailor the best experience of Rome.

History

While pursuing her PhD in Art History, our founder Nina Marok realised that her true passion is not the academy career. To the contrary, she found out that it is organising events that make people happy is her true fulfillment. She started as an Italian tour guide working for major tour operators in Italian cities and, as a result of her talent and commitment, soon she was able to build her own reputation in the sector.

Even though being a part of a big company has its benefits, it often means that the service you provide lacks a depth of a human touch. So, she decided to start Sublime Tours, a tour operator that will always value the true idea of hospitality before anything else. It did not take long before she found the rest of us - other guides who share the same values, best local chefs, coolest restaurant owners and even the administrative support.

Our philosophy

The main point is in the fact that we decided to see our customers as our guests rather than mere clients. When you are on vacation in a foreign country, you often feel that you are spending a lot of your valuable time trying to find out what the best experience might be, sometimes even thinking that you could be having much better time if you had family or friends to show you around. What if that is not impossible? Well, this is where our approach excels.

We believe that your best experience in Italy starts from the moment you land on this page. We can make it easy for you. You will easily get all the information you need. You can stop worrying about the little details that sometimes present a stone in your shoe. In all our tours and cooking classes in Rome we successfully removed all unnecessary moments with the goal to provide you with the best experience of Rome and Italian lifestyle ever. Without any further thoughts you can think of us as your warm welcoming friends.

Motivation

Yes, it is true that the work of many people is often motivated by their income. Here, at Sublime Tours, we prefer to see our personal satisfaction with all the things we did as the most important aspect of our work.

With a lot of dedication and commitment, we were able to maintain it for many years. And we will keep doing so for many years to come.

Cost And Prices

Although it might sound counter intuitive when you hear it for the first time, it is true that we are not even trying to expand business in size - we are happy with our regular amount of work. It enabled us to maintain highest quality control while offering the best possible services to our guests. While making all of our guests happy we were able to keep our prizes in the fairest possible range. You can easily find out about all costs and prices of each individual tour on their respective pages.
