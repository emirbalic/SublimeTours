Walking Tour


Welcome to our Walking tour in Rome!


This is going to be a very special day for you. We did our best to show you the heart of Rome in the best way possible. It is going to be both entertaining and educational. During the walking tour you will see the most impressive pieces of art that ever existed and hear all about the intriguing history behind them.


On an average day, walking average pace, you visit a lot of spectacular sites like Pantheon , Fontana di Trevi , Piazza Venezia , Fori Imperiali , Campidoglio , Campo de Fiori , Fontana Dei Quattro Fiumi , La Scalinata di Trinita dei Monti.


Our guides will treat each one of you as a unique individual and will adopt to your individual needs. Ask questions about anything, they will not give you just the answer but an experience to remember. If you already had a walking tour in Rome and know a lot about it, here you will talk to an expert and will find out about any detail that you might not be familiar with.


They will show you the best coffee place in Italy, the best Gelateria, the top branch restaurants in Rome. And yes, of course, you will get all the info about it so you can find it on your own later.


Besides all of that, for those who are visiting Rome for the very first time this tour in a heart of Rome will provide a valuable orientation experience.


For those who have already seen the major sights of Rome our guides will make sure that you see all of it with a new eyes.
