Walking Tour And Pasta Class


Welcome to our Walking and pizza class in Rome!


Well, if you are anything like us, then you like pasta. And if you like pasta, then you are on the right place. As simple as that.


There are stories, novels, poems and yes, there are even songs about pasta. And yet, there isn’t a single commonly accepted answer to the question why we like pasta. However, one thing is for sure. Pasta is Italian. Or better, pasta comes from Italy. It does not belong to Italians exclusively, but we all agree that Italian masters are absolute world champions when it comes to pasta.


Authoritative sources claim that there are over 600 pasta shapes. And nobody can even tell how many different types of pasta sauce exist out there. Be that as it may, in the end, it all comes to a very few most common ones. And, when we talk about the ways how to prepare it, the list is even shorter.


Best chefs are going to tell you that there is only ONE way. The way they make it.


So, we found top of the class pasta professionals in Rome for you. And they agreed to teach you their secrets. During this class you will make your own pasta from scratch with possible options like gnocchi, fettuccine or scialatelli. You will get all their tips and tricks on how to get that perfect texture of the dough and perfect tasting sauces. Because of the endless variety of options we have chosen the three most typical pasta sauces in Rome (cacio e pepe, carbonara and amatriciana). All the ingredients will be as fresh as they can ever be.


By the way, do you personally know a pasta chef from Rome? Well, if you don’t, we can assure you that those guys are the most entertaining people in business.


Further on, you will also learn how to make it by making your own dessert (tiramisu ili ciambelline di vino rosso - depending on your itinerary). You will also make an appetiser (antipasto), and you will have one drink included. Just to mention, for people wishing to learn about the gluten free pasta we do have an offer as well.


How can it ever be more entertaining?


Well, why not take a walk in the heart of Rome and get into a true mood to make pasta? Our guide will take you to the walking tour and show you some of the most beautiful sites in Rome. You will hear about the history of Piazza Navona, Column of Marcus Aurelius, Campo de Fiori, Piazza Farnese to name a few. You will hear about the great history behind each one of them.


We are sure that this is going to be the best day of your vacation in Rome.
