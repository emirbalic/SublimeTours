Walking And Pizza Class


On this tour, you will get to feel the vibe of the city and you make your own pizza at the end of the tour.

You will stroll down the streets of Rome while listening to the stories behind the squares, monuments, fountains and the biggest works of art that make Rome 'an open-air museum'. After the walk, you will visit one of the local restaurants where a professional pizza-master will teach you how to make your own pizza from scratch. Whether you have just arrived in Rome or you have already seen the major sites, this tour makes a great experience meeting the city in a different way.


Departure times:

Morning: 10 am

Afternoon: 2:30pm and/or 4pm

Meeting point:

Piazza Barberini
