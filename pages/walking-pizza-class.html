Walking Tour And Pizza Class


Welcome to our Walking tour and pizza class in Rome!


Why not combine fun and useful? Why not learn cool things when on vacation? No good answer to this one.


Everybody knows pizza. And very few people say that they don’t like it. Sure, there are zillion ways to learn how to make pizza, from online videos to recipe websites. But, if you have already tried to learn it like that, then you probably know that it is not as good as the one you get in a local pizza hut. And it is definitely not even close to the one that you had when you were in Italy.


Here, at our Walking and pizza class you finally have a chance to learn it properly.


A top of the class pizzaiolo will show you how, and guide you every step of the way as you are making your first pizza. You will learn how to make a pizza from scratch, secrets of the perfect texture of the dough and how to make toppings of your choice. To make it more interesting, your guide will tell you all the myths and legends of Italian pizza. True history of pizza. You will learn about different types based on region. After the class, you will get all the recipes and even the photos and videos taken during your class. A drink and a desert are included.


Can we make it even more interesting?


Sure, just to warm you up and get you into a true Roman mood, before the class you will have a walking tour where you can see some of the major attractions in Rome. Piazza di Spagna, Fontana di Trevi, Pantheon.., to name just a few of them.


Your guide, who is going to you assist you you class, will make it the best day of your vacation.
