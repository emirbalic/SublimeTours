Food Tour


Welcome to our Food tour in Rome!


Italian food. That’s an institution for itself. It has a long history. There is Mediterranean sea all around the Italian peninsula that feeds the mainland with the most precious minerals by its winds and rains. It would be far too ambitious even to think about covering all of the Italian delicacies, so here we concentrate on Roman food that we are offering in our Food tour. But, that’s why you are here, aren’t you?


Roman cuisine is very rich in taste. Sometimes is made of the most common ingredients and sometimes nobody wants to even think about preparing a meal without a special ingredient coming from a different region. It is a result of thousands of years of an advanced civilisation combined by numerous sources that influenced it throughout millenniums.


While we are all familiar with typical Italian dishes such as pizza and gelato, only a few non-Italians have ever heard of suppli, saltimbocca alla Romana or maritozzo.


On this tour you will taste what we considered to be absolute top of the tops. Your Roman guide will accompany you from small restaurants that offer the most delicious street food to tasty bites coming from a traditional salumeria. This is a lifetime opportunity to learn not only what Romans eat and drink but also how they do it.


As Italian food is never complete without wine, so the tour includes tastings of three local wines and/or liquors. Of course, you will get a taste of best coffee, pasta, and gelato as well.


Our guides are experts in everything that tour has to offer. But, on this food tour you are probably not going to ask many questions, for you will be enjoying this powerful experience.


Our food tour in Rome will also give you a chance to find about those hidden passages within the heart of the city. Depending on the itinerary you could see Campo di fiori, Piazza Farnese, Piazza Testaccio or Trastevere while visiting all the stops. It took us years to develop those paths that connect all the stops where you can enjoy the tastiest food on the Earth.


Recommended by so many, waiting for you to check out why this tour is our best seller and a five star award winning tour in Rome.
